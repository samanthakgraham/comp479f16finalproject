Staff

Departmental Assistant - Judy Thykootathil

Office: S-LB 901
Phone: 848-2424 ext 3223                       
Email: judy.thykootathil@concordia.ca


Responsibilities:

  • screens, processes and directs incoming mail, e-mail, phone and in-person inquiries for the main office 
  • answers general departmental inquiries such as student advising, Math Help Centre hours, faculty office hours
  • prepares curriculum submissions and undergraduate/graduate program calendar updates 
  • prepares course outlines and solution manuals for all courses 
  • receipt and distribution of teaching evaluations 
  • maintains departmental databases (courses, photocopier charges, etc) 
  • coordinates office hours for all instructors, and class cancellations 
  • prepares correspondence, course and research materials, examinations, grant applications, etc. for full- and part-time faculty, as well as for researchers/visiting professors 
  • acts as a liaison for departmental research centres/activities (prepares expense reports and technical reports, and organizes conferences and seminars) 
  • room bookings for the u/g lab (LB 915-4) 
  • work orders or calls work centre for any issues regarding departmental space (lights, temperature, cleanliness, etc.) 
  • assists with key control 
  • Moodle access 
  • distributes calculator stickers 
  • orders and distributes office supplies; prepares course folders for marking 
  • Posts general information

Undergraduate Program Assistant - Rima Baroudi

Office: S-LB 901-4
Phone: 848-2424 ext 3222                      
Email: ugrad.mathstat@concordia.ca


Responsibilities:

  • answers all undergraduate program/student inquiries
  • informs students on procedures for academic re-evaluations and changing grades, deferring final exams and resolving exam conflicts
  • prepares student advising schedules and maintains the inforrmation on the web page
  • updates information for all undergraduate program pages on the departmental website
  • prepares statistical information on undergraduate students and program alumni
  • preparation of midterms and final exams; liases with the Exams Office regarding the final exam schedule
  • maintains course overfills for pre-university courses
  • maintains student grades and exams; arranges for students to view final exams; processes academic re-evaluation requests
  • coordinates textbook submissions and orders, maintains book inventory
  • organizes student orientation sessions
  • prepares correspondence, course and research materials, examinations, grant applications, etc. for full- and part-time faculty, as well as for researchers/visiting professors
  • distributes calculator stickers
  • posts u/g program information 

Graduate Program Assistant - Marie-France Leclere

Office: S-LB 901-5
Phone: 848-2424 ext 3250
Email: MF.Leclere@concordia.ca      


Responsibilities:

  • answers all graduate program/student inquiries
  • sends information to prospective students and informs on admission requirements
  • informs graduate program students about courses, grants/fellowships, financial support, academic deadlines and thesis committees
  • maintains graduate student files and related database
  • processes grades for all graduate courses as well as graduate student requests
  • assists the Graduate Program Directors in the preparation of acceptance/refusal letters of admission
  • prepares statistical information on graduate program students and program alumni
  • prepares teaching assistant contracts and monitors TA budget
  • ensures graduate program information on course schedules is correct
  • updates information for all graduate program pages on the departmental website
  • organizes graduate program committees and events (thesis defences, comprehensive examinations, student presentations and orientation sessions); transcribes and/or records minutes of meetings
  • prepares correspondence, course and research materials, examinations, grant applications, etc. for full- and part-time faculty, as well as for researchers/visiting professors
  • bookings for the Math Help Centre room (LB 912) and the graduate seminar room (GM 300-24)
  • posts graduate program information 

Assistant to the Chair - Geraldine Ford

Office: S-LB 901-9
Phone: 848-2424 ext 3232
Email: g.ford@concordia.ca                      


Responsibilities:

  • answers all inquiries related to the Chair's Office
  • maintains the Chair's agenda
  • ensures administrative deadlines and procedures are met according to the critical path
  • composes routine correspondence for the Chair and Department Administrator
  • organizes faculty recruitment activities
  • compiles and maintains departmental and faculty databases on courses, fax and telephone charges; prepares statistical reports
  • organizes departmental meetings and gatherings, conferences and seminars; transcribes minutes of meetings
  • assists in the maintainance of the departmental web site
  • inputs and formats research material, grant applications and examinations
  • maintains the departmental filing system and key control; gives out keys for exam cabinets
  • room bookings for the conference room (LB 921-4) and for common marking
  • posts general information and notices of seminars and conferences; keeps bulletin boards up to date

Department Administrator - Jane (Gianna) Venettacci

Office: S-LB 901-7
Phone: 848-2424 ext 3234
Email: jane.venettacci@concordia.ca


Responsibilities:

  • manages the daily operation of the Department; advises the Chair of relevant issues and actions undertaken
  • analyzes and approves all expenses from departmental budgets; manages trust accounts and endowment funds
  • advises the Chair of CUFA/CUPFA Collective Agreement deadlines and ensures that deadlines are met
  • initiates, establishes and implements policies and procedures, as well as assigns work to and directly supervises non-academic staff; advises regarding TRAC employees; ensures that the provisions of relevant collective agreements are followed
  • participates in departmental Tenure/Personnel/Chair Advisory/Council/Teaching committees; executes decisions arising from them
  • researches, writes, and prepares reports, analyses and correspondence for the Chair
  • manages departmental space/facilities; assigns use of space
  • organizes and supervises major projects (space renovations, special events, etc)
  • creates and maintains the departmental website and departmental databases
  • manages course offerings and teaching assignments
