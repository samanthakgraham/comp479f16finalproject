Research

Our Department is recognized for the reputation of its members in a
variety of fields, many devoting much of their time organizing internal/external events and research-related seminars.  

Areas in which departmental members are particularly active include: 

  • Actuarial & Financial Mathematics
  • Analysis & Geometry
  • Dynamical Systems & Applied Mathematics
  • Mathematics Education
  • Mathematical Physics & Differential Geometry
  • Number Theory & Computer Algebra
  • Probability & Statistics

There is also much emphasis on applications in other fields such as Biology, Finance,
Mathematical Computing, Image Recognition and Signal Processing.
