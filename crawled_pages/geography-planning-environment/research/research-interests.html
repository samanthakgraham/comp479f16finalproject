  • Rosemary-Claire Collard

    • 514-848-2424, ext. 2054
    • S-H 1255-33
    • rosemary.collard@concordia.ca

    Animal life under capitalism; commodification, value & politics of nature/life; wildlife trade, conservation & management; feminist, economic & animal geographies; political ecology & critical theory
  • Silvano De la Llata

    • 514-848-2424, ext. 5481
